About the Legendary Axeman

He is a Swedish guitarist who first became known in the 1980s for his neoclassical playing style in heavy metal, and has released 22 studio albums in a career spanning over 40 years. In August 2009, Time magazine named Malmsteen No. 9 on its list of the 10 best electric guitar players of all-time. not too bad at all!

In early 1982, Mike Varney of Shrapnel Records, who had heard a demo tape of Malmsteen's playing from the 1978 demo titled Powerhouse, brought Malmsteen to the United States. He played briefly with the band Steeler on its 1983 self-titled album. Then in one of the most amazing debut albums of all time, Malmsteen appeared with Graham Bonnet in the band Alcatrazz, playing on its 1983 album No Parole from Rock 'n' Roll. It was a formidable and sublime performance. The quality of the riffs and guitar solos was unparalleled and breathtakingly original. As Malmsteen was classically inspired he brought a new fresh sound to guitar playing which combined edgy hard rock and the melodious arrangement of notes. Graham Bonnet's unique and gritty vocal style and his thought-provoking lyrics combined with Malmsteen's powerful performance to create albums with unforgettable and dynamic songs.
